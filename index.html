The typical Pacific Northwest Summer starts abrubtly in late June and lasts well into September. This period of rare warmth and sunlight in a mostly-grey-year-round climate is highly anticipated by most residents. Since the temperature change can be extreme many residents go to water sources to cool off. Here are some options if you live in Seattle and surrounding areas:

Jump in a lake

Get soaked at the fountain

Slide into a pool
