The typical Pacific Northwest Summer starts abrubtly in late June and lasts well into September. This period of rare warmth and sunlight in a mostly-grey-year-round climate is highly anticipated by most residents. Since the temperature change can be extreme many residents go to water sources to cool off. Some options include jumping in a lake or river, cooling of at sprayparks with your kids or visiting a public swimming pool.
